🗄 IPFS-Gate

Encrypted IPFS hosting, gated by Hive payment. Files are encrypted in your browser — this gate only ever stores ciphertext. Same account, same uploads as the v4call app.

⚠ Proof of concept — not production software. For tinkerers who accept the risks.
Concept design — in active development. Servers can be rebooted, turned off, or replaced with no warning at any time. Please only use TEST tokens, free at the faucet: https://faucet.v4call.com/

Moderation

Ban = the account's uploads end (identity kill). Takedown = a CID is removed for everyone and can't come back (content kill). Delete pin = end ONE account's claim on ONE CID, leaving other hosts alone.
